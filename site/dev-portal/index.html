Foley Developer Portal¶

Goals¶

Ideally, the developer portal is one of the few places that will require your attention under normal operating parameters. As a result, the portal should be a canonical source and a single pane of glass that:

  • Extends our IDEs
    • Noise-reduced observability tooling for ops systems: Build Pipelines, high-level infrastructure metrics, logging, testing status, code quality, and (eventually) developer metrics1

  1. Metrics are meant to be used as a mechanism to understand where there are opportunities to offer our colleagues support. We treat them with care and use them to help make decisions about what new platform features should be prioritized.
